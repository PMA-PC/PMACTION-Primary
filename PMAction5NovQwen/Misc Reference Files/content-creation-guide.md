## 1. PMA Philosophy & Brand Voice

### Core Theme: "Can't Never Could, Be Better"

PMAction is built on the concept of **Positive Mental Action** - the idea that mental wellness comes through consistent, actionable steps forward, not passive hope or wishful thinking.

### PMA Variations (Use Throughout Platform)

Rotate these affirmations based on context:

**Achievement-Focused:**
- "Powerful Mindset Achieved"
- "Personal Mastery Ascended"
- "Peak Mental Achievement"
- "Potential Manifested Always"

**Progress-Focused:**
- "Progress Makes Advancement"
- "Progress Mobilizes Action"
- "Positive Momentum Amplified"
- "Practice Makes Accomplished"

**Action-Focused:**
- "Purposeful Mindful Action"
- "Persistent Mindful Action"
- "Pure Motivation Activated"
- "Profound Mindful Awareness"

**Mindset-Focused:**
- "Positive Mental Attitude"
- "Peak Mental Acuity"
- "Pure Mindful Awakening"
- "Profound Meaning Attained"

**Motivation-Focused:**
- "Persist, Motivate, Achieve"
- "Powerful Motivations Arise"
- "Pure Motivation Ascends"

### Brand Voice Guidelines

**Tone:** Warm, empowering, evidence-based, never preachy

**Language:**
- Use "you" and "your" (direct address)
- Active voice over passive
- Short, punchy sentences mixed with longer explanations
- Technical terms explained simply
- Never condescending or dismissive of struggles

**What We ARE:**
- Scientifically grounded
- Action-oriented
- Compassionate and validating
- Realistic about challenges
- Hopeful without toxic positivity

**What We're NOT:**
- "Just think positive!" toxic positivity
- Overly clinical or detached
- Judgmental about struggles
- Making promises we can't keep
- Dismissive of professional help needs

### SEO Optimization Checklist

For every blog post:

- [ ] Title: 50-60 characters, includes primary keyword
- [ ] Meta description: 150-160 characters, compelling summary
- [ ] H1: One per page, includes keyword
- [ ] H2/H3: Descriptive, keyword-rich subheadings
- [ ] Internal links: 3-5 links to related articles/challenges
- [ ] External links: 2-3 links to cited sources (open in new tab)
- [ ] Images: Alt text with descriptive keywords
- [ ] URL slug: Short, keyword-focused (e.g., `/articles/breathing-exercises-anxiety`)
- [ ] Schema markup: Article schema with author, date, medical review
- [ ] Readability: Flesch Reading Ease score 60-70
- [ ] Word count: 800-2000 words (varies by topic)

### Medical Accuracy Requirements

**For treatment/advice content:**
- MUST cite peer-reviewed journals or medical institutions
- Include publication year
- Link to source (PubMed, journal website)
- Medical review by licensed professional
- Disclaimer about not replacing professional care

**Example Citation Formats:**

*In-text:*
"According to research published in the Journal of Clinical Psychology (2023), cognitive behavioral therapy shows a 60-70% success rate for treating anxiety disorders[1]."

*Reference:*

## 3. Challenge Design Framework

### Challenge Structure Template

Every challenge needs:

**1. Overview**
- Title (clear, actionable)
- Category (mindfulness, relationships, habits, etc.)
- Duration (7, 14, 21, 30 days)
- Difficulty (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
- Age groups (teen, young-adult, adult, senior)
- Description (2-3 paragraphs, evidence-based)
- Learning objectives (3-5 bullet points)

**2. Daily Tasks (One per Day)**

Each task must include:

```yaml
Day X:
  title: "Specific, Action-Oriented Title"
  instructions: |
    Clear, step-by-step guidance
    Estimated time: X minutes
    Materials needed: [list]
    
    1. First step with detail
    2. Second step with detail
    3. Third step with detail
    
    Tips:
    - Helpful tip 1
    - Helpful tip 2
  
  reflection_prompt: "Thought-provoking question for journaling"
  
  resource_links:
    - title: "Related Article"
      url: "/articles/relevant-article"
    - title: "Scientific Study"
      url: "https://pubmed.gov/..."
  
  estimated_minutes: 15
  
  post_activity_mood_prompt: true
```

Structure days to build progressively:

- **Days 1-2:** Introduction, foundational concepts

For longer challenges:
- **Week 1:** Foundation
- **Week 2:** Practice & Refinement
- **Week 3:** Advanced Techniques
- **Week 4:** Integration & Habit Formation

## 4. Quiz & Assessment Creation

### Assessment Types

**1. Validated Clinical Screeners**
- PHQ-9 (Depression)
- GAD-7 (Anxiety)
- ASRS (ADHD)
- PSS (Perceived Stress Scale)

**Requirements:**
- Use exact validated questions (cannot modify)
- Include proper scoring interpretation
- Cite validation studies
- Include disclaimer about screening vs. diagnosis

**2. Wellness Check-Ins**
- Custom questions about overall wellbeing
- Can be modified for PMAction brand
- Should still cite research where applicable

**3. Knowledge Quizzes**
- Educational quizzes (e.g., "Test Your Mental Health IQ")
- Fun, engaging, shareable
- Include explanations for correct answers

- Suicide-related: suicide, kill myself, end my life, not worth living

## 8. Content Quality Standards

### Content Review Checklist

**Before Publishing Any Content:**

- [ ] **Medical Accuracy**
  - All claims cited with sources
  - Sources from peer-reviewed journals or medical institutions
  - Publication dates within last 5 years (unless landmark study)
  - Medical disclaimer included if advice-giving

- [ ] **Accessibility**
  - Flesch Reading Ease score 60+ (8th-9th grade level)
  - Headings properly structured (H1 ‚Üí H2 ‚Üí H3)
  - Alt text on all images
  - Color contrast meets WCAG AA standards
  - No reliance on color alone to convey information

- [ ] **Actionability**
  - Every article has clear "Your Positive Mental Action" section
  - Steps are specific and measurable
  - Links to relevant challenges/tools
  - Journaling prompts included

- [ ] **Sensitivity**
  - Person-first language ("person with depression" not "depressed person")
  - Non-stigmatizing terminology
  - Validates reader's experience
  - Doesn't minimize struggles
  - Appropriate trigger warnings if discussing trauma, self-harm, etc.

- [ ] **SEO Optimization**
  - Target keyword identified
  - Keyword in title, URL, H1, first paragraph
  - Meta description compelling and keyword-rich
  - Internal links to 3+ related articles
  - External links to cited sources
  - Image alt text includes keywords

- [ ] **Engagement**
  - Hook in first paragraph
  - Conversational tone
  - Examples and scenarios
  - Pull quotes or callout boxes
  - Visual breaks (images, lists, subheadings)
  - Clear call-to-action

- [ ] **Legal & Ethical**
  - Medical disclaimer if applicable
  - Privacy policy compliance
  - No guarantees or promises of cure
  - Crisis resources readily available
  - Appropriate when to seek professional help guidance

### Content Update Schedule

**Quarterly Review (Every 3 Months):**
- Update statistics and research citations
- Check for broken links
- Review and refresh examples
- Update recommended challenges (if new ones added)

**Annual Review (Every 12 Months):**
- Full content audit
- Medical review by licensed professional
- SEO keyword research and optimization
- User feedback integration
- Accessibility audit

### User Feedback Integration

**Collect Feedback:**
- Post-reading ratings (1-5 stars)
- "Was this helpful?" yes/no
- Post-reading mood log
- Comments (if enabled)
- Time on page (analytics)
- Completion rate

**Use Feedback To:**
- Identify outdated or confusing content
- Find gaps in coverage
- Prioritize content updates
- Improve recommendation algorithm
- Measure content effectiveness (mood improvement correlation)
