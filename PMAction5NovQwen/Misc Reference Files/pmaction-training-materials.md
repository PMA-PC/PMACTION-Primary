# PMAction Training Programs Database

## Overview
This document provides the complete content for 7-day and 30-day training programs that can be added to the PMAction app. Each program includes daily activities, prompts, and tracking mechanisms.

## 7-DAY PROGRAMS

### Program 1: Mindfulness Journey

**Target:** Stress reduction, present-moment awareness
**Difficulty:** Beginner
**Time Commitment:** 10-15 minutes daily

#### Daily Activities

**Day 1: Introduction to Mindfulness**
- **Activity:** 5-minute guided breathing exercise
- **Prompt:** "What do you notice about your breath right now?"
- **Task:** Set aside 5 minutes in your day for quiet observation
- **Journal:** "How did it feel to slow down?"

**Day 2: Body Scan Meditation**
- **Activity:** 10-minute body awareness practice
- **Prompt:** "Starting from your toes, notice any sensations without judgment"
- **Task:** Perform a body scan when you wake up or before bed
- **Journal:** "Which parts of your body hold the most tension?"

**Day 3: Mindful Breathing**
- **Activity:** Box breathing (4-4-4-4 pattern)
- **Prompt:** "Focus only on the breath - in for 4, hold for 4, out for 4, hold for 4"
- **Task:** Practice 3 rounds of box breathing
- **Journal:** "How did controlling your breath affect your state of mind?"

**Day 4: Walking Meditation**
- **Activity:** 10-minute mindful walk
- **Prompt:** "Notice each step - heel, ball, toe. Feel the ground beneath you"
- **Task:** Walk slowly for 10 minutes, focusing on physical sensations
- **Journal:** "What did you notice that you usually miss?"

**Day 5: Mindful Eating**
- **Activity:** Eat one meal with full attention
- **Prompt:** "Notice colors, textures, tastes, and the experience of chewing"
- **Task:** Eat without screens or distractions
- **Journal:** "How was this different from your usual meals?"

**Day 6: Loving-Kindness Meditation**
- **Activity:** Send well-wishes to yourself and others
- **Prompt:** "May I be happy, may I be healthy, may I be at peace"
- **Task:** Practice for 5 minutes, extending kindness to loved ones
- **Journal:** "How did it feel to wish yourself well?"

**Day 7: Reflection & Integration**
- **Activity:** Review your week of mindfulness
- **Prompt:** "What practices resonated most? Which will you continue?"
- **Task:** Create a personal mindfulness routine
- **Journal:** "What changed in your week? What will you take forward?"

### Program 2: Building Healthy Communication

**Target:** Relationship skills, conflict resolution
**Difficulty:** Intermediate
**Time Commitment:** 15-20 minutes daily

#### Daily Activities

**Day 1: Active Listening Practice**
- **Activity:** Have a 10-minute conversation where you only listen
- **Prompt:** "Listen to understand, not to respond"
- **Task:** Ask clarifying questions without adding your own story
- **Journal:** "What did you learn by just listening?"

**Day 2: "I Feel" Statements**
- **Activity:** Practice expressing feelings without blame
- **Prompt:** "I feel [emotion] when [situation] because [reason]"
- **Task:** Rewrite 3 "you always" statements as "I feel" statements
- **Journal:** "How does this change the message?"

**Day 3: Identifying Triggers**
- **Activity:** Notice what situations make you reactive
- **Prompt:** "When do I get defensive or shut down?"
- **Task:** List 5 communication triggers
- **Journal:** "What patterns do you notice?"

**Day 4: Taking Breaks Effectively**
- **Activity:** Practice the pause when emotions run high
- **Prompt:** "I need a 20-minute break. Let's return to this at [time]"
- **Task:** Use the phrase if needed, or practice saying it aloud
- **Journal:** "Why is it hard to take breaks during conflict?"

**Day 5: Expressing Needs Clearly**
- **Activity:** State what you need without criticism
- **Prompt:** "What I need from you is..."
- **Task:** Write down 3 unmet needs and how to ask for them
- **Journal:** "Why don't you ask for what you need more often?"

**Day 6: Validation Practice**
- **Activity:** Validate someone's feelings even if you disagree
- **Prompt:** "I can see why you'd feel that way given..."
- **Task:** Validate one person today before offering your perspective
- **Journal:** "How did they respond to being validated?"

**Day 7: Communication Check-In**
- **Activity:** Reflect on the week's practices
- **Prompt:** "Which skill was hardest? Which helped most?"
- **Task:** Set one communication goal for next week
- **Journal:** "What communication pattern do you want to change?"

### Program 3: Gratitude Practice

**Target:** Positive mindset, appreciation
**Difficulty:** Beginner
**Time Commitment:** 5-10 minutes daily

#### Daily Activities

**Day 1: Three Good Things**
- **Activity:** List 3 things that went well today
- **Prompt:** "What am I grateful for right now?"
- **Task:** Write them down before bed
- **Journal:** "Why did these things matter to you?"

**Day 2: Gratitude for People**
- **Activity:** Think of someone who helped you
- **Prompt:** "Who made my life better recently?"
- **Task:** Message them a thank you
- **Journal:** "How did expressing gratitude feel?"

**Day 3: Gratitude for Challenges**
- **Activity:** Find the lesson in a difficulty
- **Prompt:** "What did this hard experience teach me?"
- **Task:** Write about one challenge you're grateful for in hindsight
- **Journal:** "How has struggle made you stronger?"

**Day 4: Sensory Gratitude**
- **Activity:** Notice 5 things you can see, hear, touch, smell, taste
- **Prompt:** "What small pleasures am I noticing?"
- **Task:** Practice 5-4-3-2-1 grounding with gratitude
- **Journal:** "What beauty exists in ordinary moments?"

**Day 5: Gratitude for Your Body**
- **Activity:** Appreciate what your body does for you
- **Prompt:** "Thank you, body, for..."
- **Task:** List 5 things your body allows you to do
- **Journal:** "How can you treat your body with more kindness?"

**Day 6: Future Gratitude**
- **Activity:** Be grateful for things coming in the future
- **Prompt:** "I'm grateful for the opportunity to..."
- **Task:** Write about 3 things you're looking forward to
- **Journal:** "How does anticipation feel different from worry?"

**Day 7: Gratitude Letter**
- **Activity:** Write a letter to someone you've never properly thanked
- **Prompt:** "You may not know this, but you changed my life when..."
- **Task:** Write the letter (you don't have to send it)
- **Journal:** "What did you realize while writing?"

## 30-DAY PROGRAMS

### Program 1: Habit Transformation System

**Target:** Building sustainable positive habits
**Difficulty:** Intermediate
**Time Commitment:** 20-30 minutes daily

#### Week 1: Foundation (Days 1-7)
**Theme:** Awareness & Selection

**Days 1-2:** Habit Audit
- **Activity:** Track all current habits (good and bad)
- **Prompt:** "What do I do on autopilot?"
- **Task:** Write down morning, afternoon, evening routines
- **Journal:** "Which habits serve me? Which don't?"

**Days 3-4:** Identify Keystone Habit
- **Activity:** Choose ONE habit that will create ripple effects
- **Prompt:** "What single change would improve multiple areas?"
- **Task:** Research your chosen habit (exercise, meditation, journaling, etc.)
- **Journal:** "Why this habit now?"

**Days 5-6:** Design Your System
- **Activity:** Plan when, where, how you'll do the habit
- **Prompt:** "After [existing habit], I will [new habit]"
- **Task:** Create implementation intention and prepare environment
- **Journal:** "What obstacles might I face?"

**Day 7:** First Week Commitment
- **Activity:** Make habit tiny and specific
- **Prompt:** "Start so small you can't say no"
- **Task:** Do the 2-minute version of your habit
- **Journal:** "How did it feel to actually start?"

#### Week 2: Consistency (Days 8-14)
**Theme:** Daily Practice & Tracking

**Daily Pattern:**
- **Morning:** Review commitment
- **Task:** Complete your habit at the planned time
- **Evening:** Log completion and note feelings
- **Journal Prompts:**
  - Day 8: "What's the hardest part?"
  - Day 9: "What's working well?"
  - Day 10: "When am I most tempted to skip?"
  - Day 11: "How is this affecting other areas?"
  - Day 12: "What excuses am I making?"
  - Day 13: "What small win can I celebrate?"
  - Day 14: "Am I being too hard on myself?"

#### Week 3: Deepening (Days 15-21)
**Theme:** Increasing Duration & Intensity

**Days 15-17:** Extend Your Habit
- **Activity:** Increase from 2 minutes to 5-10 minutes
- **Prompt:** "I'm ready to do more"
- **Task:** Gradually extend habit duration
- **Journal:** "How does progress feel?"

**Days 18-20:** Add Variety
- **Activity:** Introduce slight variations to prevent boredom
- **Prompt:** "How can I make this more enjoyable?"
- **Task:** Try different approaches within the same habit
- **Journal:** "What version do I like best?"

**Day 21:** Milestone Celebration
- **Activity:** Reflect on 3 weeks of consistency
- **Prompt:** "I've done this for 21 days. What's changed?"
- **Task:** Reward yourself meaningfully
- **Journal:** "What proof do I have that habits work?"

#### Week 4: Mastery (Days 22-30)
**Theme:** Automation & Expansion

**Days 22-25:** Identity Shift
- **Activity:** Start thinking "I am a person who..."
- **Prompt:** "This is just who I am now"
- **Task:** Tell someone about your new habit
- **Journal:** "How has my self-perception changed?"

**Days 26-28:** Stack Another Habit
- **Activity:** Add a second tiny habit
- **Prompt:** "After [habit 1], I will [habit 2]"
- **Task:** Link a new 2-minute habit to your established one
- **Journal:** "Can I handle two habits?"

**Days 29-30:** Future Vision & Commitment
- **Activity:** Plan your next 30 days
- **Prompt:** "If I keep this up for a year, what happens?"
- **Task:** Write your 90-day and 1-year habit goals
- **Journal:** "What habit do I want to build next? What did this month teach me?"

### Program 2: Relationship Reset (30 Days)

**Target:** Improving primary relationship or family dynamics
**Difficulty:** Advanced
**Time Commitment:** 20-40 minutes daily

#### Phase 1: Self-Work (Days 1-10)

**Days 1-3:** Identify Your Patterns
- **Activity:** Notice your reactive patterns in conflict
- **Prompt:** "What's my go-to response when threatened?"
- **Task:** Track: Do you fight, flee, freeze, or fawn?
- **Journal:** "Where did I learn this pattern?"

**Days 4-6:** Understand Your Attachment Style
- **Activity:** Research attachment theory (secure, anxious, avoidant)
- **Prompt:** "How do I typically behave in relationships?"
- **Task:** Take an attachment style quiz, read about yours
- **Journal:** "How does my attachment style show up?"

**Days 7-10:** List Your Triggers & Needs
- **Activity:** Deep dive into what sets you off and why
- **Prompt:** "What unmet need is beneath my reaction?"
- **Task:** Create two lists: Triggers & Underlying Needs
- **Journal:** "What do I actually need from my partner/family?"

#### Phase 2: Communication Skills (Days 11-20)

**Days 11-13:** Practice "I Feel" Statements
- **Activity:** Reframe complaints into feeling statements
- **Prompt:** "I feel ___ when ___ because ___"
- **Task:** Practice 5 different scenarios in writing
- **Journal:** "How does this reduce defensiveness?"

**Days 14-16:** Reflective Listening
- **Activity:** Repeat back what you heard before responding
- **Prompt:** "What I'm hearing is... Is that right?"
- **Task:** Use this technique in one conversation daily
- **Journal:** "How did the other person respond?"

**Days 17-18:** Request, Don't Demand
- **Activity:** Turn criticism into specific requests
- **Prompt:** "Would you be willing to...?"
- **Task:** Make 3 requests instead of complaints
- **Journal:** "What makes asking hard?"

**Days 19-20:** Appreciation Practice
- **Activity:** Express genuine gratitude daily
- **Prompt:** "I really appreciated when you..."
- **Task:** Thank your person for something specific each day
- **Journal:** "How does this change the dynamic?"

#### Phase 3: Repair & Reconnection (Days 21-30)

**Days 21-23:** Apologize Effectively
- **Activity:** Own your part without "but"
- **Prompt:** "I'm sorry for... That must have felt... Next time I'll..."
- **Task:** Make one genuine apology
- **Journal:** "Why is apologizing vulnerable?"

**Days 24-26:** Schedule Connection Time
- **Activity:** Create ritual connection moments
- **Prompt:** "When can we connect without distractions?"
- **Task:** Schedule 30-minute weekly check-ins
- **Journal:** "What gets in the way of quality time?"

**Days 27-28:** Try Something New Together
- **Activity:** Share a new experience
- **Prompt:** "What could we try that we've never done?"
- **Task:** Plan and do one new activity together
- **Journal:** "How did novelty affect our connection?"

**Days 29-30:** Future Vision Together
- **Activity:** Discuss relationship goals
- **Prompt:** "What kind of relationship do we want to build?"
- **Task:** Share your vision and listen to theirs
- **Journal:** "What did I learn about our shared hopes? What's my next 30-day relationship goal?"
